Weyland-Yutani Corporation

"Building better words"

Weyland-Yutani Corporation stands as a global powerhouse in the realms of technology and manufacturing, with offices spanning Earth, Thedus, and Luna's Sea of Tranquillity. Specializing in the production of synthetics, spaceships, and cutting-edge computers, Weyland-Yutani serves industrial and commercial clients alike, while also dominating interplanetary shipping and transport.

Our Services

Terraforming

Since 2137, our Atmosphere Processing Plants have successfully terraformed hundreds of extrasolar planets and moons with inhospitable atmospheres.

Bioengineering

Our bioengineering department specializes in harnessing the unique biological properties of many intergalactic species, including Xenomorph XX121.

Synthetics

Our synthetics, like the David and Walter models, perfectly mimic humans in appearance and behavior while boasting superior strength, speed, and coordination.

Transport

Our interplanetary shipping and transport services, in partnership with the Interstellar Commerce Commission, ensure safe and efficient trade across the cosmos.
